About me

2020-11-27

Hi friends, I’m Eric.

In my 20-ish year professional life, I’ve been an astrophysicist, defense researcher, data scientist, software developer, manager, and startup co-founder. At home, I’m a husband, dad, video gamer, and occasional guitar noodler. I get pleasure out of asking questions and learning new things, and I love to share what I’ve learned.

My mission here is to write about subjects I enjoy in a way that I think might be somewhat engaging and accessible. Topics you’ll likely encounter include physics, astronomy, math, programming, data, probability and statistics, and machine learning. So, technical stuff, mostly. While I’ll try to keep the writing conversational, expect to see lots of math and code.

My hope is that people (including me!) reading these posts will gain new insights and maybe even take the first steps on a longer path of discovery. This is very much an experiment in expression, reflection, sharing, and a bit of self-care. I don’t know where this will lead, but I’m excited for the journey.
